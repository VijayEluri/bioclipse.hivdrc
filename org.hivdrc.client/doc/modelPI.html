Protease Inhibitor model

This proteochemometric model is described in Lapins et al., BMC Bioinformatics (2008) 9:181, and allows for prediction of drug susceptibility of seven clinically used PIs from the HIV protease sequence. The model is based on 828 mutated HIV protease genome sequences and the experimental susceptibility data for the seven protease inhibitors measured by their ability to inhibit the replications of the respective mutated HIV variant in vitro; in total the model is based on 4794 protease sequence-inhibitor combinations. For more information, please see:

M. Lapins, M. Eklund, O. Spjuth, P. Prusis, and J.E.S. Wikberg.
Proteochemometric modeling of HIV protease susceptibility
BMC Bioinformatics (2008), 9:181, doi:10.1186/1471-2105-9-181
